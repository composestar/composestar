Step 2 - Download StarLight source from Sourceforge

To download the StarLight source from Sourceforge you need to have a SVN client installed (e.g. Turtoise SVN, http://tortoisesvn.tigris.org/)

Use your SVN client to download the following folders from Sourceforge:

  • trunk/Core (https://svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/composestar/trunk/Core)
  • trunk/StarLight (https://svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/composestar/trunk/StarLight)

Note: do a svn switch on the Core folder to the branches/StarLightCore (will be merged somewhere in the future)
